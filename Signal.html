Radio waves and Signal work togeather.

Radio waves are a type of electromagnetic radiation and travel at the speed of light. A signal is generated by lightning and other astronomical objects.The electricity flowing through the antenna causes the electrons floating through to vibrate up and down and when the radio waves arrive at the antenna the electrons inside of it vibrate which produces an electric current that recreates the original signal. The modulation signal output by the demodulator is amplified to increase its strength and the information are then converted back to a human-usable form by a transducer.
